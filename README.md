## About Me

I am a junior undergraduate student at the **School of Physics, Peking University**. My research lies at the intersection of **artificial intelligence** and **natural sciences**, with a focus on:

- **AI for Biology** -- Applying reinforcement learning to biomolecular design (e.g., T-cell receptor optimization)
- **AI for Physics** -- Benchmarking and building LLM agents for computational imaging and inverse problems
- **AI Agent Systems** -- Developing autonomous agents capable of scientific reasoning and computation

I am passionate about building AI systems that can accelerate scientific discovery across disciplines.
